About me

In 2022, Fliss was living on a small farm in West Auckland, surrounded by native bush and a seed was planted. With a background in healthcare management and MBA majoring in Sustainability, she set up a company to make tech solutions supporting climate action, measuring how many Good Humans out there are already doing the right thing. Our team is made of volunteers from all over the world, brought together by the idea that if we all do a little, it adds up to a lot. Starting with growing what you can, where you can, right now. You can find out more about our Team here. PlantMe is designed to make it easy to get growing. Whether you have space for some pots, a garden, or the opportunity to be stewards of friends and family land or allotment space, growing more of your own food, veg, fruit, nuts, flowers, natives can help save the planet, one plant at a time. We hope you love using PlantMe because a lot of love went into building it. If you have suggestions or thoughts on how we can make it even more awesome
