Discription

Crassula ovata, commonly known as jade plant, lucky plant, money plant or money tree, is a succulent plant with small pink or white flowers that is native to the KwaZulu-Natal and Eastern Cape provinces of South Africa, and Mozambique; it is common as a houseplant worldwide.

Tips

Grow in very bright light indoors. Plant jade plants in Miracle-Gro® Cactus, Palm & Citrus Potting Mix. Water when the top 1 to 2 inches of soil are dry. After a month, feed jade plants when you water with Miracle-Gro Succulent Plant Food. Prune off dead or shriveled branches.
